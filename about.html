BUS MAESTRO


DBMS Mini Project

The goal of the bus management system is to offer a systematic method for managing the depot admin, drivers, and users.

The Depot administrator can access all the data, add drivers and user{students or faculties}, and allocate them trips thanks to the bus Maestro. also students can upgrade their payment directly to the collegeg via BUS MAESTRO. The driver can input the actual time of departure and arrival as well as the amount of fuel used.

As a result, the BUS MAESTRO makes it simple to operate and administer the organisational bus transportation services hassle free.


Database Description

BUS

Each bus will have a unique bus number & integrated gps .

ADMIN

The depot administrator can add new Students & faculties/drivers by assigning them their credentials, which include their respective ID and password. The administrator can also assign trips. The bus number, source, destination, scheduled arrival, departure, STDNT/FCLTY ID, and driver ID must all be entered by the administrator. The trip number, which is the primary key, is auto generated.

STUDENT AND FACULTY

When a student or faculty signed-in, he can see the bus number he was used to enter, as well as the current location of bus and students can pay their fee and view corresponding details .

DRIVER

Similarly When the driver logs in, he can see the trip number, as well as the bus number . The driver can enter trip information such as fuel consumed, actual arrival and departure times, and kilometres driven.
